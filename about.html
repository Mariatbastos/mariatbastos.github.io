ABOUT HER

Maria Bastos is a fashion and graphic designer.

Having graduated in 2011 at Faculdade de Arquitetura, in Lisbon, with a Fashion Design degree, Maria moved immediately to London, where she started interning and assisting at Wonderland magazine and stylists.

Quickly after this experience, Maria joined a design team and started her journey together with the creative teams, she began assisting and then became head designer for Working for Aquascutum Golf.

In 2014 she joined a creative agency company, where she worked with British brand: Crew Clothing, Aquascutum Women in the UK and Hong Kong, Chinty and Parker, Brompton Bikes. At this agency, together with the team, they created a Men's trend book that was bought by high profile clients.

Six years later, in 2015, she moved back to Lisbon and helped set up 2 kidswear brands, Miau and Moki&Mar, and freelance with a new luxury phone company, Connexion D'art. In 2017 she joined a new womenswear brand, BOON CLOTHES, as the head designer where she stayed for 2 years until Maria moved to New York City.
